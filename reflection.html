John 3:16 “For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have eternal life.”

I believe that putting God first—“Una ang Ginoo”—helps me make good choices every day. It guides how I treat others, how I learn, and how I act online. I try to live with honesty, kindness, and faith in everything I do. Thinking about my actions helps me grow as a person. I try to improve myself every day by learning from my mistakes and doing what is right.
